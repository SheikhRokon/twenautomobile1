About Us

TWEEN Automobile School

TWEEN Automobile School is the leading Professional Automotive Training institute in Bangladesh. We are offering multiple ranges of skill development training for the Automobile Industry, Including Basic Automobile Engineering, Advanced Automobile Engineering, Compute Diagnosis, and troubleshooting of Vehicle, Hybrid, and Electric Vehicle Technology, Fleet Management, and Operation. Our Instructors are highly skilled. They have more than six (06) years of experience in the Automobile Industry. They are especially experts in Mitsubishi, Suzuki, Mahindra, ISUZU, Swaraj-Mazda, and SMLI.
